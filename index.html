You know your business. We know data.

We help you use data as a resource to address your business challenges.
Here's how we do it.

  • Emphasis on Value

    Businesses of all sizes inevitably spend time and resources solving data related problems only to realize the final product doesn't actually help the business. We practice the human-centered design process of planning, building, testing, and iterating to incrementally deliver valuable insight every step of the way.

  • Strategic Priority

    All problems are not created equal. We help you prioritize strategic data initiatives in order to tackle the low-hanging fruit and avoid common pitfalls. We work with you and your team to understand your pain points, imagine all that is possible, evaluate what is feasible, and develop an execution plan.

  • Analytics Expertise

    Vast data sources and a well defined business strategy are useless without the know-how to analyze them. We bring deep expertise in data mining, statistical modeling, business intelligence, and visualization to make insights accessible - no matter the size or type of data.

  • Solutions not Tools

    No single tool is right for every job. We utilize a tool-agnostic approach to properly evaluate all options and identify the best path forward. With a wide range of technical expertise, we design a tailored product that is built to drive business impact regardless of your technical environment.

  • Agile Transparency

    No one knows your business better than you. Data projects are vastly more impactful when augmented by deep business domain expertise. We work in open collaboration with our client teams to identify and execute valuable opportunities that drive tangible business outcomes.

  • User Experience

    A solution is only as beneficial as the user perceives it to be. We draw upon our graphic design experience to create a solution that not only delivers insight, but does so in a way that is intuitive to the individual users it is crafted to support.
